Financial Technology

In a Finance and Technology Hackathon, we presented a financial techonology solution in 24 hours. We proposed a risk evaluation platform for Indian banks who could lend money to farmers but without data support

The Delfi solution is an oracle into the world of lending risk. With Delfi, the lending institution has access to a comprehensive profile of each potential lender like never before. By using an expert-crafted cocktail of data metrics, the lender is presented with the risk percentage associated with the potential borrowing customer in a smooth dashboard style interface. The Delfi synthesizes data metrics specific to the customer in order to generate a lending risk, which can be used by the lender to make strategic, informed decisions of to whom to lend.

There are three important feature of Delfi. Firstly, it uses comprehensive data such as weather patterns, regional access to technology and transportation, international crop prices to determine a more comprehensive data analysis to determine a borrower’s financial risk. Secondly, the models and algorithms are evolving. In other words, this solution becomes smarter and more accurate each day. Thirdly, its simple API enables financial institution to harness all the analysis power.
